Help & User Guide

Welcome to the PEAK Assistant! This tool guides you through a structured research and hypothesis workflow. Hereâ€™s what you need to know for each phase:

1. Research Phase
  • What to do: Enter a research topic or question you want to investigate.
  • What happens: The assistant will gather and summarize relevant information.
  • Your role: Review the generated research report for accuracy and completeness.
2. Hypothesis Phase
  • What to do: Use the research report to generate possible hypotheses.
  • What happens: The assistant will suggest hypotheses based on the research.
  • Your role: Select or edit a hypothesis to move forward with.
3. Refinement Phase
  • What to do: Refine your chosen hypothesis for clarity and testability.
  • What happens: The assistant will help critique and improve your hypothesis.
  • Your role: Accept, further edit, or re-refine the hypothesis as needed.
4. ABLE Table Phase
  • What to do: Generate an "ABLE Table" to plan how to test your hypothesis.
  • What happens: The assistant will create a table outlining Actions, Barriers, Levers, and Evidence.
  • Your role: Review and adjust the table to fit your research plan.
Tip: You can always return to any phase using the sidebar navigation. For best results, provide clear and specific input at each step.
